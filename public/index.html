Our vision

To bring tech-savvy people together to learn, share knowledge and connect across the world. We work as a unit, striving to create a proficient future for our youth.

Team Members

Most of the team members are students from colleges all over India joining us to make this event a huge success. The hard work and dedication of our team since the beginning were remarkable.

What is DevCon?

DevCon is a tech-based summit that will provide you with the chance to learn about various cutting-edge technologies and their implementation. It will be a two-day-based summit, organized in March 2022, that will bring together notable analysts, developers, tech enthusiasts, and industry professionals who will share their ventures, ideas, and loads of knowledge.
